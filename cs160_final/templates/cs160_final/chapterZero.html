Steve is an accountant who spends most of his time performing data entry. One day, his boss forces him to work all night and show up to work the next day.



Steve decides that enough is enough. He wants to summon demons to do his accounting work for him. While they work for him, he can work on improving his personal life by attending all of the great accounting conferences and not having to work overtime in order to input all of the data.
